Spotify User Data Tool

The tool on this page allows you to see data about your Spotify account. Currently, it is capable of accessing your 'liked songs' playlist and allowing you to download said playlist in .csv format (as a backup or if you are moving to another streaming provider for example). PLANNED => Access your followed artists and tell you if they have any shows near you in the future.
